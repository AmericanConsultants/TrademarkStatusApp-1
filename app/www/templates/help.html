Help
Search
Use trademark Serial Number, Registration Number or Reference Number to perform searches. You can search for multiple trademarks by using comma as a separator. Search Format -
Serial Number - Number with 8 digits
Registration Number - Number with 5 to 7 digits
Reference Number - Alphanumeric with 8 characters
Swipe search results from right to left to share or bookmark them. Click on an item in search results to bring up the trademark detail page.
Notebooks
Create Notebooks to group your favorite trademarks. Notebooks can be swiped to delete them. Clicking on a notebook will bring up all trademarks grouped under that notebook.
Bookmarks
Click on a notebook to access Bookmarks page. Swipe an item in the list to delete or share bookmark and click on it to see trademark details.
Notifications and Alerts
The system keeps monitoring the status of trademarks that you've bookmarked. Whenever a status change is detected, it alerts you with a notification.
Clicking on the notification will bring up 'Alerts' page in the app. This page lists all trademarks whose status has changed. You can swipe on an item to dismiss and remove it from this page. The 'Alerts' page can also be accessed from the Notebooks tab by clicking on the Alert icon.
Preferred Status Codes
There are about 150 possible status codes for a trademark. To receive notifications only for specific status changes, select your preferred status codes on this page.
